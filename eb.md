### **3. Timeline and Process (Jan 2025 Context)**

**CRITICAL WARNING:** As of the January 2025 Visa Bulletin, the EB-2 category is **backlogged** for all countries (including "Rest of World").

#### **Scenario: Filing in January 2025**
*   **Step 1: File Form I-140 (Immigrant Petition).**

*   **Step 2: Wait for Priority Date (The Backlog).**
    *   You **cannot** file Form I-485 (Green Card) or apply for an EAD/Advance Parole in Jan 2025 because your Priority Date (Jan 2025) will not be "Current."
    *   **Current Cutoff (Jan 2025 Bulletin - Estimate):** The "Date for Filing" for ROW is likely stuck in **2023**.
    *   **Wait Time:** You will likely wait **12–18 months** *after* I-140 approval before you can file your I-485 (depending on visa movement).

*   **Travel & Work:**
    *   **Travel:** You can travel on your current visa (F-1/H-1B) *only if* that visa allows it. Filing an I-140 alone does *not* grant travel benefits, nor does it strictly invalidate non-immigrant intent for some visas (H-1B/L-1 is dual intent; F-1 is not). **If you are on F-1, travel after filing I-140 is risky.**
    *   **Work (EAD):** You **cannot** apply for an EAD based on the NIW until your Priority Date is current and you file the I-485. You must rely on OPT, STEM OPT, or H-1B in the meantime.
